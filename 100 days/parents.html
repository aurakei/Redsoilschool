Uniform

At Redsoil School we want our pupils to be members of our school and wear our uniform (including PE Kit). The uniform has been designed to be practical for a primary school while reflecting the high standards expected at school â€“ it is to be worn with pride at all times. We feel that it is important that children learn to dress smartly and appropriately and grow up at RedSoil School understanding that most schools and later in life when they are working they will be required to dress smartly and appropriately. We look to parents/carers to work with the school to ensure that children come to school always maintaining a high standard of dress. It is our policy that all pupils should wear school uniform (including PE kit) when attending school, or when participating in a school-organised event outside normal school hours (unless there are different dress requirements). Management

The school is managed by highly qualified staff. The structure is composed of ; THE CHAIRMAN, DIRECTORS, ACCOUNTS MANAGER, THE HEAD TEACHER , THE TEACHERS , THE SUPPORT STAFF and THE SECURITY DEPARTMENT. The staff work as a team to ensure that every child is treated with care and utmost comfort to ensure they have acquired the desired knowledge and skills. We have EXAM CARDS, TRANSPORT PASSES, MEAL CARDS and STUDENT ID CARD so that every child is accounted for and gets the full service from the school.
