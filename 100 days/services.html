our services

Transportation

We offer transport depending on your zone. We operate within 3 zones (refer to Fee structure). Our transport staff are qualified, friendly and very reliable. We have four main trips in a day, spread out as per your zone.

Zone 1: Redsoil, Mashurie, Nalepo, Rimpa

Zone 2: Kiserian, Nkoroi 1, Olosurutia, Maxwell, Brookhurst.

Zone 3: Naromoru, St. Patrick’s, Nkoroi II, Laiser Hill, Kandisi, Matasia, Rongai.

Extra-Curricular Activities

>> Our students are grouped into four houses - RED HOUSE, GREEN HOUSE, YELLOW HOUSE and BLUE HOUSE.

>> We have Home Science club, Business club, Aerobics club, Indoor games- (chess, scrabble, draft, cards), P.P.P club (people, places, plants), drama club, music club, fashion and modeling, art and craft.

>> We have sports including ; football, net ball, volley ball and tug of war, athletics.

>> We take our students to exposure-excursions, trips and camps.

Meals

Our diet ranges from White Rice, Pilau, Minced meat, steamed cabbage, Kales, Sorghum porridge, tea, red beans and potatoes. We also have special diets for children with special needs. We serve two meals, tea Break and Lunch.
