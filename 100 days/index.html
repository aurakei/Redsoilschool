Mission

Established on a Christian foundation, Redsoil school aims at providing an ideal learning environment for the growth and development of well balanced God-fearing children. The school provides affordable quality education meant to provide affordable quality education meant to produce Kenyans equipped with knowledge, skills and positive attitudes to overcome the challenges of the fast changing and modern world.

George, Director

Running a learning institution is not a walk in the park, neither is it a normal business like others where focus is on profit, increase of sales and management of expenses. Heading a learning institution in this case Redsoil School has been a journey of remarkable growth both internally and externally. When a child comes into our institution at age 4 and leaves the institution in class 8 having completed 11 years in school, consistently gaining knowledge and building a foundation for their future purpose, then one looks back and concludes that this is actually a calling. This has been the reality of my experience at Redsoil School. I celebrate our God, The LORD Jesus Christ and appreciate His Strength, guidance and wisdom as my colleagues and I navigate through this exciting experience in giving parents good service to their children and to the community at large. Indeed we continue to pursue our vision which remains to establish a strong deep mental, physical and spiritual foundation for every child who passes through our hands. We believe with God’s favor, this will be achieved as we fulfill our God given Purpose. Asante.
